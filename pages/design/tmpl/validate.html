Validation Resources

Here are several links to mobile validation or page analysis tools related to mobile design. Use them to gage how well your website fits established mobile parameters.

We have found many of them useful in identifying problems, but not always reflecting fully obtainable levels of compliance in the real world. In other words, they are great guides.
