OSM MediaElementPlayer.js

Our MobileSiteOS Integrated Version of MediaElementPlayer for video file playback.

This page demo's the MediaElementPlayer with MobileSiteOS integration, playing either .webm, .mp4 or .ogv video files. The correct video is rendered according to the user's browser, and displayed at the correct page size. And player controls are also tailored to the displaying browser, based on device size and capabilities.
